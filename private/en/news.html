July 31, 2025 Publication

APL Machine Learning Paper on Materials Maps

Our team published a paper in APL Machine Learning demonstrating a graph-based materials map that fuses experimental and computational data to accelerate the discovery of thermoelectric materials. The work highlights an AI workflow capable of screening vast datasets and prioritising promising candidates for laboratory validation.

October 27-30, 2025 Conference

Keynote Lecture at IMPRES2025

Yusuke Hashimoto will deliver a keynote lecture at the 4th International Meeting on Prediction of Structure and Properties (IMPRES2025). The talk will introduce a machine-learning driven approach to map thermoelectric materials by integrating experimental observations and high-throughput calculations.

JSAP Autumn Meeting 2025 Presentation

Our group will report the latest progress on materials informatics for spintronics at the 86th JSAP Autumn Meeting. The session will cover data-driven screening workflows that shorten iteration cycles between simulation and experiment.
