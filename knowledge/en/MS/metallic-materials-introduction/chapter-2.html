2.1.1 Phase Rule（Gibbs of Phase Rule）

thermodynamicsEquilibriumstatein、system of degrees of freedom determinationdoBasiclawGibbs Phase Ruleis。

Gibbs Phase Rule：

$$F = C - P + 2$$

where、

  • $F$: degrees of freedom（temperature, pressure, compositionetc of numbernumber of）
  • $C$: componentnumber（elements number of）
  • $P$: Phasenumber of（phases number of）

2component system（$C = 2$）、one （ 1 ） of case、 fixeddofordegrees of freedom is 1 ：

$$F = C - P + 1 = 2 - P + 1 = 3 - P$$

example：

  • Single-Phase Region（$P = 1$）: $F = 2$ → Temperature and Composition of can be
  • Two-Phase Region（$P = 2$）: $F = 1$ → Temperaturewhen determined、eachPhase of Composition one determine
  • Three-Phase Coexistencepoint（$P = 3$）: $F = 0$ → Temperature also Composition also fixed（Invariant Point）
flowchart TD A[Gibbs Phase Rule: F = C - P + 2] -->B[2component system, one] B -->C[F = 3 - P] C -->D[Single-Phase Region P=1] C -->E[Two-Phase Coexistence P=2] C -->F[Three-Phase Coexistencepoint P=3] D -->G[F = 2: Temperature・Composition] E -->H[F = 1: Temperaturewhen determinedComposition determine] F -->I[F = 0: Temperature・Composition and also fixed]

2.3 Phase Transformation of thermodynamics

Phase Transformation（phase transformation） is 、Temperature and of changeby、existPhasefrom of Phase to and changedo is。Phase Transformation understanddo is 、GibbsFree Energy of concept Importantis。

2.3.1 GibbsFree Energy

GibbsFree Energy of ：

$$G = H - TS$$

where、$G$: GibbsFree Energy、$H$: entarupi 、$T$: Temperature、$S$: entropi

Equilibriumstatein、system of GibbsFree Energy Minimum 。Temperature changedo and 、 also GibbsFree Energy Phase stablePhase and 、Phase Transformation 。

2.3.2 Phase Transformation of

Phase Transformation occurfor of is 、2Phase of GibbsFree Energy is。

$$\Delta G = G_{\beta} - G_{\alpha}$$

$\Delta G<0$ of when、αPhasefromβPhase to of transformation thermodynamic is。
