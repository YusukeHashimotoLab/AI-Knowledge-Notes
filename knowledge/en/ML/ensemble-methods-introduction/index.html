Series Overview

Ensemble Learning is a powerful machine learning technique that improves prediction accuracy by combining multiple models. It achieves performance beyond single models through diverse approaches: reducing variance with bagging, reducing bias with boosting, and combining heterogeneous models with stacking. Modern gradient boosting methods like XGBoost, LightGBM, and CatBoost are overwhelmingly popular in Kaggle and real-world ML competitions.
