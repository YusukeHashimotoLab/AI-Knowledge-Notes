3.1 Angular Momentum Operators and Commutation Relations

ğŸ“š Angular Momentum Algebra

Angular momentum operator: \(\hat{\mathbf{L}} = \hat{\mathbf{r}} \times \hat{\mathbf{p}}\)

Fundamental commutation relations:

\[ [\hat{L}_x, \hat{L}_y] = i\hbar\hat{L}_z, \quad [\hat{L}_y, \hat{L}_z] = i\hbar\hat{L}_x, \quad [\hat{L}_z, \hat{L}_x] = i\hbar\hat{L}_y \]

Ladder operators:

\[ \hat{L}_\pm = \hat{L}_x \pm i\hat{L}_y \]

\[ \hat{L}_\pm |l,m\rangle = \hbar\sqrt{l(l+1) - m(m\pm 1)} |l, m\pm 1\rangle \]

Eigenvalues:

\[ \hat{L}^2 |l,m\rangle = \hbar^2 l(l+1) |l,m\rangle, \quad \hat{L}_z |l,m\rangle = \hbar m |l,m\rangle \]

Summary

In this chapter, we studied angular momentum and spin:

  • Angular momentum algebra: Commutation relations, ladder operators, eigenvalues
  • Spin 1/2: Pauli matrices, Bloch sphere, measurement probabilities
  • Angular momentum addition: Clebsch-Gordan coefficients, triplet and singlet states
